About My Workflow

Continuous Innovation

I am **always working on different projects simultaneously**. I don't believe in stagnation; the moment I finish one project, I am already architecting the next. This constant cycle of creation helps me stay ahead of the curve.

Security Focus

Recently, I have **started learning Cyber Security**. My goal is to evolve from building apps that *work* to building apps that are **secure, resilient, and unbreakable**. This focus is integrated into my development lifecycle.

AI-Augmented Workflow

I **leverage the power of advanced AI models** to accelerate my development. By integrating AI into my workflow, I save time on boilerplate code and focus entirely on **complex logic and system architecture**.

Strategic Planning

Before writing the first line of code, I focus on system architecture and scalability. This **strategic planning** ensures that every project is built on a solid, future-proof foundation, minimizing technical debt.
